Bangla License Plate Recognition

Our system is capable of de-blurring motion blurred and speedy vehicle images of Bangladeshi license plates. All you have to do is to upload an image and we will decode the license plate for you.

Why use our system?

Road accidents have now become a great social concern in Bangladesh. As Government is installing CCTV cameras in roads, our system will be able to detect license plates from speedy vehicles. Mostly, the system will benefit to prevent traffic law violations, such as hit and run and speed limit breaks.

Our system will substantially help in crime investigations. No need to use expensive high-speed cameras. our system will be able to detect license plates with the use of decent CCTV cameras in low-cost.
